Who We Are

Sein Diamond is an industry leader

Sein Diamond Paint is the most experienced of all Myanmar paint companies. Starting in 1979, Mr. Tin Latt began Sein Diamond as one of the first domestic paint companies established. Sein Diamond has everthing you need to make your house a modern masterpiece. Sein Diamond makes dozens of exterior paints and other accessories, formulated and tested to stand up to the rigours of Myanmar's monsoon and other extreme climates.
